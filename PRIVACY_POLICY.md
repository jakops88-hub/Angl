# Angl Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-04

**1. Data Collection:** Angl operates strictly on-device. All image processing and pose analysis occur locally using Google ML Kit. We do not collect, store, or transmit your biometric data to any external servers.

**2. Camera Usage:** Camera access is required strictly for real-time composition analysis and taking photos at your request.

**3. Storage:** Photos are saved directly to your device's local Gallery (MediaStore). Angl does not maintain cloud backups.

**4. Third-Party:** We use Google ML Kit for pose detection. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
